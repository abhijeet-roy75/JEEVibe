Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! The Free tier is completely free forever. Use it to experience JEEVibe's adaptive learning before deciding to upgrade.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and wallets through Razorpay - India's most trusted payment gateway.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What's the difference between Pro and Ultra?

Pro gives you more daily limits (10 snaps, 10 quizzes), unlimited chapter practice, offline mode, and full analytics. Ultra removes all limits and includes access to Priya Ma'am, your AI tutor who can help you understand concepts deeply.

Do solutions quality differ by tier?

No! All tiers get the same high-quality, step-by-step solutions. We believe every student deserves complete explanations. The difference is in usage limits and features.

Is there a student discount?

Our annual plans offer up to 33% savings. We've priced JEEVibe to be accessible to all serious JEE aspirants. Perfect for coaching class students who want to maximize their learning.

Can I use JEEVibe with my coaching class?

Absolutely! JEEVibe is designed as a companion app for coaching class students. Use it to practice what you learn in class, get instant doubt-solving, and track your progress between coaching sessions.
