# Privacy Policy for JEEVibe

**Last Updated**: December 5, 2025

## Introduction

JEEVibe ("we", "our", or "us") operates the JEEVibe Snap and Solve mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Camera Permission
The App requests camera permission to enable the core "Snap & Solve" feature, which allows you to:
- Take photos of JEE exam questions
- Submit these images for automated solution generation

### Data Collection
- **Images**: Photos of questions you capture using the camera
- **Usage Data**: Questions solved, practice progress, and accuracy statistics
- **Device Data**: Basic device information for app functionality

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Process Questions**: Analyze captured images using OCR and AI to extract questions
- **Generate Solutions**: Provide step-by-step solutions and explanations
- **Track Progress**: Maintain your daily snap count and practice statistics
- **Improve Service**: Enhance our AI models and question recognition accuracy

## Data Storage and Security

- **Temporary Storage**: Images are temporarily stored on your device and sent to our secure backend server for processing
- **No Long-Term Storage**: Question images are not permanently stored on our servers after processing
- **Secure Transmission**: All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
- **Local Storage**: Your progress data is stored locally on your device

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **OpenAI API**: For question recognition and solution generation
- **Cloud Hosting**: Render.com for backend infrastructure

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your stored progress and statistics
- **Delete**: Clear your local data by uninstalling the app
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the camera feature at any time

## Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for students preparing for JEE exams (typically ages 16+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- **Question Images**: Deleted immediately after processing
- **Progress Data**: Stored locally until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **No Account Data**: We do not collect account or personal identification information

## Camera Permission Usage

The camera permission is used exclusively for:
- Capturing images of JEE exam questions for the Snap & Solve feature
- No photos are taken without your explicit action
- No background camera access
- Photos are only processed when you actively use the feature

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
